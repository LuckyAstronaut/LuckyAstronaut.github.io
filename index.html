How Badly Luck Can Fuck You Over

NASA is currently hiring 10 astronauts for a new space program, Artemis. Including you, there are 5,000 applicants. You are the most qualified out of everyone applying. Will you be chosen? Let's find out. Below, select the minimum qualification score required to apply. We will assign each applicant a random qualification score from the minimum to 100, pretending that they are evenly distributed, for the most part. Your qualification score will always be 100. As well, select how much weight luck will have on the outcome, out of 100. You will have completely average luck at 50%. If you want, you can also change the number of applicants. Now run the simulation. Good luck!
